Send Template to Important Users

Send WhatsApp template messages to the most important and active users who are outside the 24-hour free messaging window.

User Importance Scoring

Users are ranked by importance score (max 250 points):

  • Has ordered before: 50 points (highest priority)
  • Total orders: 5 points/order (max 50)
  • Total spending: 1 point/1000 spent (max 30)
  • Messages sent: 1 point/10 messages (max 20)
  • Days active: 2 points/day (max 30)
  • Reviews written: 5 points/review (max 20)
  • Referrals made: 10 points/referral (max 30)
  • Recommendation acceptance: up to 20 points
